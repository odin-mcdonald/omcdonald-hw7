Fun Facts!

Tool

The discordant sounds at the start of the song “Disgustipated” were created by the band smashing up and hitting three pianos and some shotguns with a sledgehammer... and the sound for the unusual short instrumental in “Mantra” was created by Maynard squeezing his cat.

Slipknot

The band refused to wash their clown masks throughout the course of their tour so they could carry the “spirit” of the tour with them wherever they went.
